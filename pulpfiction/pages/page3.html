MIA

Vincent and Jules arrive at Marcellus Wallace's club with the briefcase. Marcellus and Butch are talking about the upcoming fight. Marcellus gives Vincent instructions to escort his wife.

Vincent goes to Lance and Jodi's house to buy some H. He also shoots up.

Vincent arrives at Mia's house. They decide to go to dinner.

He takes her to Jack Rabbit Slim's where they participate in the twist contest. The two finish eating and go back to Mia's house.

Back at the house, Vincent goes to the bathroom while Mia rifles through his coat pockets, finding the (then unknown) bag of heroin. She snorts it and overdoses.

Vince carries her into his car. He takes her to Lance and Jodi's, crashing into their house in the process. Lance and Jodi freak out before deciding they need to shoot her with an adrenaline needle. Vincent stabs her. She's revived.

He takes her home and the two agree to never speak of the incident again.
