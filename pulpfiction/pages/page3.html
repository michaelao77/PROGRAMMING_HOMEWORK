MIA

Vincent and Jules arrive at Marcellus Wallace's club with the briefcase. Marcellus and Butch are talking about the upcoming fight. Marcellus gives Vincent instructions to escort his wife.

After shooting up at Lance and Jodi's house, Vincent arrives at Mia's house. He takes her to Jack Rabbit Slim's where they participate in the twist contest. The two finish eating and go back to Mia's house.

Back at the house, Vincent goes to the bathroom while Mia rifles through his coat pockets, finding the (then unknown) bag of heroin. She snorts it and overdoses. Vince finds her and takes her to Lance and Jodi's where he shoots her with an adrenaline needle. Once revived, the two agree to never speak of the incident again.
