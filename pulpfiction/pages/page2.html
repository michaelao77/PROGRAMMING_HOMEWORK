THE DINER

Jules and Vincent are eating breakfast at the diner. Pumpkin and Honey Bunny are sitting nearby and decide to rob the place.

Vincent goes to the bathroom. Pumpkin and Honey Bunny begin the hold-up. They reach Jules and try to take the briefcase. Jules takes out his gun.

Vincent comes out of the bathroom, sees what's going on and also pulls his gun out. Jules gives them his money, but keeps the briefcase. The conflict is reseolved.
