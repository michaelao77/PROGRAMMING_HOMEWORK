THE PAWN SHOP

It's the night of the fight. Butch accidentally kills his opponent. He flees to Fabienne's apartment. The next day, they are planning on leaving for Mexico. Fabienne forgets Butch's gold watch. He goes back to his apartment to retrieve it.

While there, he notices a gun on the counter. Then a flush from the bathroom. Vincent comes out of the bathroom and Butch, with gun in hand, shoots him dead. He leaves. On the way back, he runs into a furious Marcellus. Marcellus shoots at him and he flees fto a pawn shop.

At the pawn shop, Marcellus and Butch are fighting each other when Maynard appears. He ties them up. Zed shows up on the Chopper. They appoint the Gimp to keep watch over Butch while they take Marcellus to the back. Butch breaks free, knocks out the Gimp, and goes back for Marcellus, sword in hand. He slays Maynard, Marcellus shoots Zed, and they agree that they're cool now. Butch rides the Chopper back to Fabienne.
