PULP FICTION

The Narrative

While the movie is famous for its non-linear narrative, today we're going to put its timeline in order.

THE BRIEFCASE

The timeline kicks off with Jules and Vincent driving to pick up the briefcase at Marvin's apartment. A shoot out occurs. On the way back, Vincent accidentally shoots Marvin dead, making a mess of the car in the process.

Jules and Vincent go to Jimmie to dispose of the body. The wolf arrives. The mess is cleaned. Jules and Vincent drive to Monster Joe's Truck and Tow, then head to the diner in a taxi, briefcase in hand.
